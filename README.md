# Carpoo

Go With The Flow

A modern ride-sharing and carpooling platform designed to make travel more affordable, social and sustainable.

Available on Google Play and Indus Appstore.

🚗 Book rides
🚘 Publish rides
🛡️ Identity verification
⭐ Trust Score System
💬 Secure Messaging
🤖 AI Assistant
📍 Smart Ride Matching

## Why Carpoo?

Every day millions of seats travel empty.

At the same time, millions of students, professionals and daily commuters spend unnecessary money on transportation.

Carpoo connects riders and drivers already travelling on similar routes, helping people save money, reduce traffic and make transportation more accessible.
